You might not be hungry...

Based on what you said, you might not actually be hungry. So we need to address the need that you have with an alternative to food.

Below are some suggestions for what you can do instead of eating. Have a look, and congrats on taking the time to be mindful about deciding whether or not to eat!

Instead of eating, try one of the following:

  • Meditate
  • Go for a walk
  • Workout
  • Journal about what might be making you stressed/anxious
  • Do some yoga
  • Take a break and go get some water

The main thing is that you address whatever the actual reason is for you wanting to eat when not hungry. And if you do ultimately decide to have something to eat, then at least do so mindfully.
